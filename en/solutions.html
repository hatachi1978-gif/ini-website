Boundaries Between the Two Tracks

Boundary Definition

What Is Shared

Common Foundational Capabilities

  • China-Europe dual-side team and time-zone coordination
  • European renewables sector understanding and local network
  • Cross-cultural project management capability
  • Awareness of compliance documentation and decision gates

What Is Not Shared

Independent Operating Systems

  • Client types and decision-making chains
  • Business processes and deliverables
  • Contract structures and business models
  • Risk logic and control methodology
  • Team composition and execution rhythm
