About INI

Not Intermediaries.
Operators on the Ground.

Iberian Nexus Investment SL is registered in Madrid, Spain, with a team in Shanghai. We specialize in cross-border execution in the China-Europe renewable energy sector, operating two independent business tracks: European market entry and localization for Chinese renewable energy companies, and cross-border investment matching between European renewable assets and Chinese capital.

Our core team brings 10+ years of full-value-chain experience in European renewables — covering PV, energy storage, and wind across development, EPC, and equipment manufacturing. Based in Madrid, operating across major European markets.

Our European partners ensure localized execution on the European side; the Shanghai team coordinates with Chinese capital and supply chains. From first introduction to first delivery, we walk every step with you — without blurring the boundaries between the two tracks, and without using one as leverage for the other.

We believe cross-border business should not be marketed as "end-to-end consulting" or an "all-in-one platform." Complexity must be decomposed into executable, staged tasks — each with a defined deliverable, a defined owner, and a defined exit criterion.

One team. Two time zones.
One cross-border execution discipline.
